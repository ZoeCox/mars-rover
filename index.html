Rover Expedition

Give the Rover commands to navigate the plateau
You can either use the buttons to command it, or you can type directions into the text field. Type "L" to turn left, "R" to turn right, or "M" to move forward
Other commands will not result in instruction to the Rover
The plateau is 10 x 10 squares and you start off at position 0, 0 in the bottom left hand corner of the plateau
You start off facing East
